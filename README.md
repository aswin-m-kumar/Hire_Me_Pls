# Hire Me Pls

Hire Me Pls is an AI-powered resume analyzer, scorer, and rewriter designed to help you optimize your resume for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). It leverages modern web technologies and AI models to provide actionable insights, bullet rewrites, and version tracking for your resumes.
